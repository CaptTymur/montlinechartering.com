Who We Are

About Montline Chartering

Montline Chartering is an independent dry bulk brokerage founded by two Master Mariners who served as commanding officers on cargo vessels. We didn't learn shipping from a textbook — we lived it on the bridge, in the holds, and through every weather the sea could throw at us.

This is our edge. We know how cargo is loaded, how vessels perform in real conditions, and what actually matters when it comes to safe and efficient fixture execution. That hands-on experience translates into better decisions for both owners and charterers.

We connect cargo with the right tonnage through direct owner relationships, real-time market intelligence, and a straightforward approach built on trust and operational expertise.

What We Do

Services

Commercial and post-fixture support for dry bulk fixtures, from cargo enquiry and vessel search to recap and voyage follow-up.

Dry Bulk Freight Brokerage

Matching cargo stems with suitable coasters, river-sea vessels, Handysize, and larger dry bulk tonnage across the Black Sea, Mediterranean, Baltic, Continent, and Caspian trading areas.

Voyage & Time Charter Negotiation

Handling the commercial track from freight indication and firm offer to recap, covering laycan windows, cargo terms, rate negotiations, and charter party follow-up for voyage and time charter business.

Post-Fixture Coordination

Keeping owners, charterers, agents, and documents aligned after fixture, including NOR and laytime monitoring, statement-of-facts review, freight invoicing support, and demurrage or despatch follow-up.

Specialization

Market Segments

Covering the full spectrum of dry bulk tonnage — from river-sea coasters to ocean-going Handysize vessels.

Coasters & River-Sea

Small-to-medium tonnage for regional trades, river-sea navigation, and short-sea shipping across inland waterways and coastal routes.

  • Single-deck general cargo vessels
  • River-sea class tonnage
  • Short-sea and coastal trades
  • Grains, steel, fertilizers, project cargo
20,000+ DWT

Handysize & Above

Ocean-going dry bulk tonnage for intercontinental trades, deep-sea grain flows, and larger parcel shipments.

  • Handysize & Supramax bulkers
  • Deep-sea and intercontinental routes
  • Bulk grains, coal, minerals
  • Large parcel and full-cargo lots
